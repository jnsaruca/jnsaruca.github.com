Paper Prototype


I created a prototype of a website for a desktop. It has a combination of blog site, GofundMe, Etsy for care package products system. When a a user go to the homepage, they can see different articles that are either trending or new. In the menu options they can choose "Countries" to discover different stories from around the world written by people from those countries. In the "themes" menu they can choose which social issue the user would like to participate in. The "Contribute" allows the user to be a writer themselves and start a fundraising for their story. The "Order" menu shows the care packages they have purchased. On the bottom of an article, users can leave a comment to ask questions or give ratings as well.
