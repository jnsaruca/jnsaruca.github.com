Design Composition 2


For my second iteration, I refined my overall Care Trade website and focused on really thinking of ways I can integrate CSS and Javascript interaction into it. I changed my idea of having a full functional website to focusing on doing my homepage since there are a lot of interactive elements in the "About us" section. It includes a lot of stuff that we learned in class and other interactions that I will learn to add into it as well such as scroll pins and scroll objects appearing. By focusing on the homepage, the users will really get to feel what my product does and how it satisfies the objectives of the conference.
